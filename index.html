Local SEO FAQ

Answers buyers look for before they contact an agency.

How fast can local SEO start working?

Some improvements can appear within weeks after technical fixes, Google Business Profile updates, and citation cleanup. Competitive rankings usually require ongoing content, reviews, and authority work.

What makes local SEO different from regular SEO?

Local SEO focuses on searches with geographic intent. It combines website relevance, Google Business Profile strength, reviews, proximity, local content, and business listing consistency.

Do I need a Google Business Profile?

Yes. For most local businesses, a complete and verified Google Business Profile is one of the most important assets for showing up in Maps and local search results.

What should we measure?

Rankings matter, but the core metrics are calls, form submissions, booked appointments, direction requests, organic traffic quality, and revenue opportunities from local search.
