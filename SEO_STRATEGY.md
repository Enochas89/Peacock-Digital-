# Peacock Digital Agency SEO Strategy

## Primary Goal

Rank Peacock Digital Agency for high-intent local SEO searches in the chosen service area, then convert that traffic into audit requests, calls, and booked consultations.

## Positioning

Primary offer:
Local SEO that moves the needle in weeks, not quarters.

Core proof themes:
- First ranking improvements within 30 days
- Weekly progress reports with plain-language actions
- Senior strategist on every account
- Google Business Profile, service pages, reviews, citations, and reporting handled together

## Required Business Inputs

These are needed before the site can be fully optimized for local search:
- Real business phone number
- Real business address or service-area city list
- Google Business Profile URL
- Primary city and secondary service areas
- Top 3 customer industries
- Real testimonials or case studies
- Real social/profile links

## Target Keyword Map

Homepage:
- local SEO services
- local SEO agency
- local SEO company
- Google Business Profile optimization

City landing pages:
- local SEO agency in [city]
- local SEO services in [city]
- Google Business Profile optimization in [city]
- SEO company for small businesses in [city]

Service pages:
- Google Business Profile optimization services
- local citation building services
- local SEO audit
- local SEO for service businesses
- local SEO for contractors
- local SEO for medical practices
- local SEO for law firms

## Site Structure

Phase 1:
- Homepage
- Local SEO Services
- Google Business Profile Optimization
- Local SEO Audit
- Reviews & Reputation SEO
- Contact

Phase 2:
- City pages for each real service area
- Industry pages for each niche with proof or expertise
- Case studies
- Blog/resource hub

## First 30 Days

- Set up Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools
- Connect analytics and conversion tracking
- Verify Google Business Profile
- Replace placeholder phone/address/service-area data
- Add final LocalBusiness schema with real NAP
- Create XML sitemap after production domain is live
- Publish 3 core service pages
- Build first city page for the main service area
- Add real testimonials and proof
- Clean up NAP consistency across major directories

## 60-90 Days

- Publish 2-4 city or industry pages per month
- Earn local backlinks from chambers, directories, sponsorships, podcasts, partner pages, and local publications
- Build review request workflow
- Add FAQ content from sales calls and Search Console queries
- Refresh pages based on ranking and conversion data

## Measurement

Track:
- Google Business Profile calls
- Website form submissions
- Organic calls
- Consultation bookings
- Local pack rankings
- Organic rankings by city/service
- Search Console impressions and clicks
- Pages that produce assisted conversions
